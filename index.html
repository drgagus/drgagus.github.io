EndoHome

An endodontist is a dental specialist who focuses on the inner parts of teeth, specifically the pulp, nerves, and blood vessels. They are trained to diagnose and treat tooth pain, infections, and other conditions affecting the tooth's interior, often specializing in root canal therapy and other procedures to save teeth.

CAMBRA

CAMBRA ( caries management by risk assessment ) is an evidence-based approach to preventing and managing cavities at the earliest stages.

ENDO CASE

AAE EndoCase is an app developed by the American Association of Endodontists (AAE) to help assess the difficulty of endodontic cases.

CARIOGRAM

A cariogram is a graphical tool used in dentistry to assess and illustrate an individual's risk of developing future dental caries (tooth decay).

ENDO DIAG

An AAE (American Association of Endodontists) diagnosis in endodontics involves evaluating both the pulp and the apical tissues.

REG ENDO

Regenerative endodontics is a biologically based treatment for permanent teeth that aims to regenerate the pulp-dentin complex.

ENDO EDU

Endodontic education provides in-depth knowledge on the diagnosis, treatment, and prevention of pulp and periapical diseases.
